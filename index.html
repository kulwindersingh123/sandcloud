Beach Blanket Bingo: The Retro Comeback

Inspired by the 1960s cult classic “Beach Blanket Bingo,” our designs blend vintage California flair with modern coastal aesthetics. Imagine yourself basking under the sun with a blanket that channels retro surf culture while keeping your skin cool and sand-free. It’s more than a throwback – it’s a movement.

Babylon Meets the Beach

Our “Babylon” collection takes its cue from San Francisco’s vibrant cultural history – especially the iconic “Beach Blanket Babylon” cabaret. We reimagined this energy in every stitch, turning each blanket into a wearable, shareable piece of art that tells a story.

Built for the Bold

Whether you’re chasing waves, chilling at a beach bonfire, or setting up a lakeside picnic, your beach blanket should roll with you. Ours are lightweight, fast-drying, and roll up neatly into your beach tote. Some even come with hidden zip pockets – because we’re all about those beach hacks.

Limited Editions, Unlimited Vibes

We drop limited collections inspired by beach destinations around the world. From Malibu to Goa, every design is handpicked by local artists, ensuring no two beach days look the same. Bonus: your purchase helps fund marine life conservation through our eco partners.

Join the Movement

We’re more than a brand – we’re a beach-loving community that cares. Every blanket sold contributes to ocean cleanups and sustainable textile innovation. So when you wrap yourself in one of ours, you’re wrapping yourself in purpose.
